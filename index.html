About Me

I am a Doctoral Candidate in Astronomical Instrumentation at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA), Bengaluru, and Calcutta University, working under the supervision of Prof. Sivarani Thirupathi.

My research focuses on ground-based exoplanet atmospheric characterization using low- and high-resolution transmission spectroscopy, instrumentation development, and advanced data-analysis pipelines.

Research Interests

  • Low- and high-resolution exoplanet transmission spectroscopy
  • Telluric, stellar (RM & CLV), and instrumental systematics
  • Multi-object observing strategies
  • Atmospheric dispersion correctors (ADC)
  • Fiber-positioning systems for ELT-class spectrographs
