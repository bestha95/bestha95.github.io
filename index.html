About Me

I am a Doctoral Candidate in Astronomical Instrumentation at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA), Bengaluru, and Calcutta University. I expect to complete my Ph.D. in August 2026 under the supervision of Prof. Sivarani Thirupathi.

My research focuses on improving the precision and reliability of ground-based exoplanet atmospheric characterization through low- and high-resolution transmission spectroscopy, instrumentation development, and advanced data-analysis pipelines.

Research Interests

  • Low- and high-resolution exoplanet transmission spectroscopy
  • Correction of telluric, stellar (RM & CLV), and instrumental systematics
  • Multi-object observing strategies for atmosphere characterization
  • Optical design of atmospheric dispersion correctors (ADC)
  • Fiber-positioning systems for ELT-class spectrographs
  • Time-series analysis and noise modeling (Gaussian Processes, SYSREM)
