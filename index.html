About Me

I am Manjunath Bestha, a doctoral researcher working on exoplanet instrumentation and transmission spectroscopy. My research focuses on improving ground-based exoplanet atmosphere studies by developing multi-object observing strategies and combining low- and high-resolution spectroscopy to reduce telluric and instrumental systematics. I work at the interface of observations, data analysis, and optical instrumentation, with the goal of enabling more precise atmospheric characterization using current and future large telescopes.
