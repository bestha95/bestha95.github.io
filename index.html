About Me

I am a doctoral researcher working on exoplanet instrumentation and transmission spectroscopy, focusing on improving ground-based atmospheric characterization through multi-object observing strategies and multi-resolution spectroscopy.

Research Interests

  • Ground-based exoplanet transmission spectroscopy
  • Multi-object observing strategies for systematics removal
  • High- and low-resolution spectroscopic techniques
  • Astronomical instrumentation and optical design
  • Observations and methodologies for ELT-class telescopes
