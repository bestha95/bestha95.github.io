About Me

I am a doctoral researcher at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics working on ground-based exoplanet atmospheric characterization. My research focuses on developing observational strategies and instrumentation concepts for multi-object and multi-resolution transmission spectroscopy.

My work combines low- and high-resolution spectroscopy, telluric correction, noise modeling, and optical design for future spectrographs.

Research Interests

  • High- and low-resolution exoplanet transmission spectroscopy
  • Multi-object observing strategies for systematics removal
  • Telluric correction and time-series noise modeling
  • Optical design and simulation of spectrographs
  • Atmospheric studies of hot and ultra-hot Jupiters
